With this Colorful Screw, make your way to the north of **Rouge Plaza**, and take the holovator across the street from the **Looker Bureau**. On this building, use your Roto-Glide to make your way to the top of the Looker Bureau, and the Colorful Screw is on the edge, overlooking the Rouge Plaza.
